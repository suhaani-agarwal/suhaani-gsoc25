About Me

Hello! I'm Suhaani Agarwal, an aspiring developer from India, and a contributor to open source through Google Summer of Code 2025.

This summer, I’m thrilled to be working with the R Project for Statistical Computing under the mentorship of Toby Dylan Hocking and Yufan Fei.

My project, titled “Advancing Animint2 – Testing, Rendering and Gallery Port”, aims to enhance Animint2, an R package that brings interactivity to ggplot2 visualizations. I'm focused on improving testing, refining layout rendering, and revamping the interactive gallery.

Through this blog, I’ll be documenting my 3-month journey — sharing progress reports, technical learnings, and personal insights along the way.

Thank you for being a part of this experience. Let’s make data visualization more intuitive and interactive, one step at a time!
