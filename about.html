About Me

Suhaani Agarwal

Hello! I'm Suhaani Agarwal, an aspiring developer from India and a passionate contributor to open source through Google Summer of Code 2025.

This summer, I'm thrilled to be working with the R Project for Statistical Computing under the mentorship of Toby Dylan Hocking and Yufan Fei.

My project, titled "Advancing Animint2 – Testing, Rendering and Gallery Port", aims to enhance Animint2, an R package that brings interactivity to ggplot2 visualizations.
